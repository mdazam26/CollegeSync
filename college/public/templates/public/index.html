About

CollegeSync is a multi-tenant academic management platform that helps streamline education operations from the ground up. Built for scalability, our platform is designed to support subdomain-based tenant routing, ensuring each institution gets a personalized experience.

Whether you're a Superadmin managing multiple institutions or a Director handling one, CollegeSync is tailored to your role.

Features

  • Multi-tenant support with subdomain-based routing
  • Dynamic class, batch, and branch management
  • Custom semester and subject configuration per branch
  • PDF generation for results and academic reports
  • Attendance tracking and report generation
  • Role-based dashboards for Director, HODs, and Professors
  • Mobile-responsive interface for access on the go
