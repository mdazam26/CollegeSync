Welcome to CollegeSync

CollegeSync is a modern, multi-tenant college management system that streamlines academic and administrative tasks. Whether you're an institution administrator, head of department, or faculty member, CollegeSync helps you efficiently manage:

  • Student records and attendance
  • Branch and semester configurations
  • Class scheduling
  • Subject management
  • PDF generation for results and reports

How to Get Started

  1. Click on the "Start CollegeSync" button below.
  2. Enter your desired subdomain (e.g., macet, test1).
  3. You will be redirected to your college's dashboard based on the subdomain.
  4. If you're a new user, use the form to create a tenant domain first via localhost.

Note: This system supports subdomain-based routing. You must use URLs like test1.localhost:8000 after setup.
